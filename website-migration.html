Why migration matters for SEO and operations

Old themes, random plugins, and half-finished pages make it hard to improve SEO. Migration gives you a cleaner, more predictable foundation.

  • Remove legacy clutter that slows down every change.
  • Organize content so service pages, locations, and FAQs are easy to find.
  • Create room for better internal links and clearer calls-to-action.
  • Document your new structure so future updates stay organized.

Static-site drafts, not auto-published websites

MBSRN generates draft content and structure in static HTML and CSS—like the page you're reading now. These drafts are meant for review, not automatic deployment.

This very page is an example of a migration artifact: a safe, standalone file that can be inspected, edited, and approved before any admin connects it to a live environment.

How MBSRN migration works

  1. 1. Ingest your existing site

    MBSRN scans your current website to capture page content, structure, and basic assets. This becomes the basis for audit and migration planning.

  2. 2. Align on requirements

    Operators specify what's in-scope for migration: which services, locations, and pages matter most, and where you want to simplify.

  3. 3. Generate draft site artifacts

    AI assists in drafting updated copy, navigation, and layouts tailored to your business. The output is a set of static files for operator review.

  4. 4. Review with humans

    Operators and owners review drafts section by section. You can adjust messaging, calls-to-action, and page structure before any publish step.

  5. 5. Approve and connect to your preferred hosting

    After approval, admins can work with your chosen implementation path to host the site. MBSRN doesn't lock you into a specific provider.

Operator control and safe publishing

Migration can feel risky. MBSRN is designed to make it feel controlled instead.

  • No automatic cutovers. There is always a manual decision point before any new site replaces your current one.
  • Versioned drafts. You can compare “before” and “after” versions of key pages.
  • Clear ownership. Admins decide who can approve drafts and manage hosting connections.

Designed for small business realities

Most owners don't have time for a six-month rebuild. MBSRN helps break migration into manageable steps tied to your SEO plan—not an all-or-nothing redesign.
