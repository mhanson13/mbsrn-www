Why so many small business websites “suck right now”

You’re not bad at your business. You’re overloaded. Most small business sites fall behind because there’s no simple, trustworthy way to connect everyday work with SEO and website improvements.

Outdated and hard to find

Old themes, missing content, and thin local pages make it hard for people to find you in search—especially on mobile.

Disconnected from SEO data

Google Search Console, GA4, and Google Business Profile often exist, but no one is turning their signals into a clear to-do list.

No safe way to improve

You want better results, but you also don’t want someone pushing random changes to production without your approval.

Operator-first workflow, from audit to safe deployment

Every improvement in MBSRN passes through clear, reviewable steps. AI assists with drafts and analysis, but humans stay in control of what ships.

  1. 1. Capture your reality

    Ingest your existing site and capture your goals, constraints, and service areas.

  2. 2. Run an SEO audit

    Gather evidence about technical health, content coverage, and search visibility.

  3. 3. Review recommendations

    See prioritized fixes with evidence, rationale, and expected impact.

  4. 4. Compare competitors

    Understand your market, content gaps, and opportunities in local search.

  5. 5. Generate migration drafts

    Produce draft content and site structures you can open, edit, and comment on.

  6. 6. Approve and publish safely

    No change reaches production without explicit operator or admin approval.
